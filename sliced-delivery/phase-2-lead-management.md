# Phase 2 — Lead Management

**Goal:** Structured lead capture, qualification, and one-click conversion to a case.  
**Estimated duration:** 1 week (Week 3)  
**Depends on:** Phase 1 (Cases list, Counters list, Flows 3/4/5 all operational)  
**Business value:** Replaces email inbox, sticky notes, and spreadsheets with a structured pipeline. Gives management visibility of lead conversion rates.

---

## What This Phase Delivers

At the end of Phase 2, the following is operational:

1. Staff can create a **Lead record** from the Staff App Leads screen.
2. A `LeadID` is auto-generated (e.g. `LEAD-0001`) on lead creation.
3. Staff can update lead status (New → Contacted → Qualified → Lost / Dormant) as the enquiry progresses.
4. When a lead is qualified, staff clicks **[Convert to Case]** — a single button in the Staff App that triggers the full conversion flow.
5. The conversion flow:
   - Creates a Case record (reusing Phase 1's Flow 3/4/5 for CaseID, Fact-Find, folders)
   - Updates the Lead with `ConvertedToCaseID` and `Status = "Converted"`
   - Sends the welcome email to the customer (fact-find link + upload folders)
6. Staff can filter and search the leads list by status, product interest, and assigned staff member.
7. The Referrers list is populated to track introducer / referral partner leads.

## Power Automate Flows Required

### Flow 1 — Generate Lead ID

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Trigger | When Lead item created |
| Action | Read Counters list (LEAD row); increment CurrentValue; update Lead Title and LeadID with `LEAD-XXXX` format |
| Dependency | Counters list (LEAD row) |
| Notes | Same optimistic locking pattern as Flow 3 |

> **Source:** `10-automation-summary.md` line 13

---

### Flow 2 — Convert Lead to Case

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Trigger | Button (Staff App — on Lead detail screen) |
| Action | 1. Create Case item (pre-populating from Lead: applicant names, email, phone, ProductType, PropertyAddress, LoanAmountRequired, LeadID, AssignedTo → CaseOwner, EnquiryDate); 2. Flows 3, 4, 5 then trigger automatically from case creation; 3. Update Lead: set `ConvertedToCaseID = [new CaseID]`, `ConvertedDate = today`, `LeadStatus = "Converted"` |
| Dependency | Flow 3 (CaseID), Flow 4 (Fact-Find), Flow 5 (folders) — all triggered by case item creation |
| Notes | The welcome email to customer is sent by Flow 5 once folder links are ready |

> **Source:** `10-automation-summary.md` line 14; `09-end-to-end-process.md` lines 30–39

## Phase 2 Acceptance Criteria

| # | Criterion |
|---|---|
| 1 | Staff can create a new lead from the Leads screen |
| 2 | LeadID is auto-generated (e.g. LEAD-0024) on lead creation |
| 3 | Staff can update lead status through the pipeline (New → Contacted → Qualified → Lost) |
| 4 | [Convert to Case] button is visible when LeadStatus = "Qualified" |
| 5 | Clicking [Convert to Case] creates a Case record pre-populated with lead data |
| 6 | Lead is updated with ConvertedToCaseID and Status = "Converted" |
| 7 | Customer receives welcome email (fact-find link + upload folders) via the existing Flow 5 |
| 8 | Converted leads are no longer shown in the active leads filter |
| 9 | Staff can assign leads to team members and filter by assigned staff |
| 10 | Referrers list is populated and can be selected on a lead (ReferrerID lookup) |
